University Management Module

This module provides comprehensive management tools tailored specifically for universities. Key features include:

  • Student Enrollment: Easily manage student admissions, registrations, and personal information.
  • Course Scheduling: Efficiently create and manage course schedules, including class times and locations.
  • Faculty Management: Maintain records of faculty members, their qualifications, and teaching assignments.
  • Examination Management: Organize and administer exams, track results, and generate reports.
  • Gradebook: Record and calculate student grades, monitor academic progress, and generate transcripts.
  • Financial Management: Track tuition fees, manage budgets, and generate financial reports.

With intuitive user interfaces and powerful functionalities, universities can streamline their administrative processes and enhance operational efficiency.
